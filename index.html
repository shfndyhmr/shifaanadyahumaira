About Me

Currently studying at Universitas Gadjah Mada, Faculty of Engineering, majoring in Geodetic Engineering (class of 2023). I have a strong interest in mapping, geospatial technology, and its applications in development and environmental sustainability.

Experienced in various competitions, including the Fachruddin Arrazi Competition (International Competition), Kompetisi Matematika Nalaria Realistik (KMNR), and Lomba Cerdas Cermat MAPSI, demonstrating strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Previously served as Secretary in the Red Cross and Rohis, showcasing excellent organizational and administrative abilities. Currently active in Keluarga Mahasiswa Teknik Geodesi (KMTG) and Unit Kegiatan Mahasiswa (UKM) Unit Penalaran Ilmiah Interdisipliner, further developing leadership, teamwork, and community engagement.
